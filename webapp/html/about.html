About EduConnect

A peer-to-peer tutoring platform built by students, for students.

Our Mission

EduConnect was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to facilitate meaningful academic connections between students who need help and those who can provide it.

We believe that peer-to-peer learning is one of the most effective ways to understand complex concepts, develop critical thinking, and build confidence in academic settings.

Our platform makes it easy for students to find peer tutors who excel in specific subjects, while also providing an opportunity for knowledgeable students to share their expertise and earn while helping others.

How It Works

Sign Up

Create your profile as a student or tutor (or both!) and list your courses and expertise levels.

Create Requests

Students can create specific help requests for courses they're taking, specifying their needs.

Connect & Learn

Tutors respond to requests, students choose their preferred tutor, and successful learning happens!
