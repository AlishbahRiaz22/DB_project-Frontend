About EduConnect

EduConnect is a revolutionary peer-to-peer learning platform designed specifically for students. We believe in the power of collaborative learning and understand that sometimes the best way to learn is from someone who has recently mastered the material.

Our platform connects students with knowledgeable peer tutors who excel in their subjects, creating an environment where learning becomes more accessible, relatable, and effective.
